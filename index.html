About Soccer

Soccer, also known as football in most parts of the world, is a globally popular sport played between two teams, each striving to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net. Played on a rectangular field, soccer involves skillful footwork, teamwork, and strategy. It captures the hearts of millions with its fast-paced action, dramatic goals, and the unity it fosters among fans worldwide.
